    Affordability and quality—close to home

    Indiana Tech is the area leader in online and on-campus education, and a quality degree from our university is among the most affordable in the Midwest.

    Thanks to generous regional and merit scholarships, grants and other financial aid programs, nearly 95% of our students receive substantial assistance in paying for college.
